Who is Okultix?

Okultix is a fictional figure from the future. My alter ego for the artist.

Okultix has no connection to the occult; he is just my excuse to creatively express myself through 3D. In a dystopian world,

Okultix thrives as a symbol of artistic rebellion. His dark and enigmatic creations explore themes of isolation and existentialism. Through his work, he challenges societal norms, inviting viewers to confront their fears and embrace the complexity of human existence.
